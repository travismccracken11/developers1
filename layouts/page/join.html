Developers

Developers are encouraged to visit the GitHub pages for any of our exciting projects. All are welcome to contribute to our meritocratic community! Contribute a project or a feature, file a bug, fix a bug, create a PR, star our projects, contribute documentation, or just hang out in our discussion channels. For technical writers, in addition to individual Project documentation, you can contribute to the main ledger documentation.

All participants are required to adhere to the Code of Conduct.

Some projects within the Hedera projects require a signed Contributor License Agreement (CLA) from each participant. The purpose of the CLA is to ensure that the Hedera projects have the necessary rights to use and redistribute your contributions as part of the project. The CLA also provides certain representations and warranties regarding your contributions.
